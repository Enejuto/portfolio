/about_me_

Welcome to my portfolio.

My name is Lucas Caswell, going by "enejuto" online. I'm a digital layout and graphic designer driven by a need to create visually appealing content. I enjoy modern, clean aesthetics, drawing inspiration from well-made, visually pleasing themes and crafting subtle palettes.

I come from an English background and have been a big drawer since childhood. Over the years, I've sharpened and refined my skills across various digital art, design & coding tools, allowing me to play around with my concepts in the goal to make something I can be proud of.

My fascination with computers began when I was around nine or ten years old, captivated by the hardware and software that made them run. But it wasn't until my teen yearss that I discovered my hobby and enjoyment from making graphic design and "buttons" - which eventually grew into interface, shifting away from a computer engineering/science path.

That hobby developed and intergrated itself into my education, shaping not just my creative identity but also the educational and career path I'm pursuing today - where design, innovation, and clean visuals meet.
